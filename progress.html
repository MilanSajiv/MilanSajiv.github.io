Targets
What I'm working toward

Visible abs

Typically visible at 10–12% body fat. Estimated 4–5 months of consistent deficit to reach this. Protein at every meal and 15k steps daily are the main levers.

Sharper face and waist definition

Typically one of the first visible changes. Reducing sodium (crisps), sugar (chocolate), and alcohol will accelerate this. Usually noticeable within 6–8 weeks of consistent dieting.

Larger shoulders, chest, back, arms

Progressive overload over 12–18 months. Hip thrusts and terminal knee extensions are priority for the knee rehab. Face pulls and rows for posture and shoulder health.

Stronger legs and calves

Knee tendon rehab is the priority — strengthening quads, glutes, and core reduces tendon load. Hack squat and single leg squat machine replacing deep barbell squat.

Improved posture

Face pulls and rows are already in every session. Dead bugs and Pallof press for core stability. The desk job makes this ongoing — not a one-time fix.

Knee pain resolved

Tendon pain addressed through structured quad, glute, and core strengthening. Terminal knee extensions and hip thrusts are the priority rehab exercises. Wearing sleeve every session.
