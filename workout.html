Fitness & Health

Workout Programme

3-day split — Mon (A), Thu (B), Fri (C) at 5am. The Foundry Gym, Warwick. Knee sleeve every session. Rehab focus: quads, glutes, core.

Monday · 5am

Day A — Lower Body + Rehab

Warm-up: 5 min treadmill walk + leg swings + hip circles

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Hack squat machine 3 × 12 Moderate depth, controlled descent
Romanian deadlift 3 × 10 Hip hinge, soft knee bend
Hip thrust (barbell or dumbbell) Priority 3 × 12 Squeeze glutes at top
Single leg squat machine 2 × 10 each Controlled — addresses left/right imbalances
Terminal knee extension (cable) Rehab 3 × 15 Face cable, loop behind knee, press to straight
Standing calf raise 4 × 15–20 Full range — heel all the way down, pause at top, slow tempo

Core Finisher (5 min)

Dead bug3 × 8 each side
Plank2 × 30–45 sec
Pallof press (cable)2 × 10 each side
Thursday · 5am

Day B — Upper Push + Glute Accessory

Warm-up: 5 min arm circles + 2 × 15 light cable rows

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Dumbbell shoulder press 3 × 10 Seated or standing
Incline dumbbell press 3 × 10 30–45 degree bench
Lateral raises 3 × 12 Light, controlled — form over weight
Tricep pushdown (cable) 3 × 12 Rope or bar attachment
Cable kickback or hip abduction machine 3 × 15 Glute accessory — do not skip

Core Finisher (5 min)

Dead bug3 × 8 each side
Plank2 × 30–45 sec
Pallof press (cable)2 × 10 each side
Friday · 5am

Day C — Upper Pull + Glute Accessory

Warm-up: 5 min shoulder rolls + light face pull warm-up set

Exercise Sets × Reps Notes
Lat pulldown 3 × 10 Full stretch at top
Seated cable row 3 × 10 Chest up, pull to lower chest
Single-arm dumbbell row 3 × 10 each Brace on bench
Bicep curl 3 × 12 Controlled on the way down
Face pull (cable, rope) Priority 3 × 15 Shoulder health — do not skip
Single-leg glute bridge (floor) 3 × 12 each Glute accessory

Core Finisher (5 min)

Dead bug3 × 8 each side
Plank2 × 30–45 sec
Pallof press (cable)2 × 10 each side

Progression Rule

When all reps are comfortable for 2 sessions in a row, add the smallest weight increment available. Do not increase because one session felt easy — confirm it twice.

Knee Monitoring

  • Reduce hack squat depth first
  • Reduce single leg squat range
  • Drop to bodyweight hip thrust
  • Never skip terminal knee extensions — these help most when the knee is aggravated

Low Energy Fallback

Skip a main exercise rather than doing it badly. Never skip the core finisher — it takes 5 minutes and is the easiest part to keep. One missed session never matters. Missing three weeks matters.
