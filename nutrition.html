Dietary
Restrictions & Swaps

Restrictions

  • Lactose intolerant — no milk, regular dairy
  • Gluten intolerant — rice-based diet
  • No fish

Key protein sources

  • Chicken breast (~31g per 100g)
  • Eggs (~6g each)
  • Clear Whey Isolate (Myprotein)
  • Turkey mince, lean beef
  • Peanut butter (snacks)

Mum cooks dinner

Home-cooked dinner most evenings. Estimate by eye — fist of rice, palm-sized protein, rest vegetables. Don't stress exact numbers on home meals.

Supplements
Stack

Clear Whey Isolate — Myprotein

No reactions confirmed — lactose intolerance, not full milk protein allergy. Mix with water. Taken pre-gym (4:30am) and post-gym or as needed to hit daily protein target.

Creatine Monohydrate — 5g daily

Taken every day including rest days — not just gym days. Timing flexible. Adds ~1–2kg on scale from muscle water retention. Not fat gain — don't panic when it goes up.
