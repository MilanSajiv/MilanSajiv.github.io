Startup Roadmap

Amaea

AI-powered FCA compliance platform for UK IFA firms. Built to become the world-leading compliance intelligence platform. The north star: a multi-billion exit.

The vision

World-leading AI compliance

Start in UK IFA compliance. Build the best AI compliance assistant in the market. Expand sector by sector. Go international. Become the compliance OS that regulated industries run on.

Robin Powell network CISI CEO relationship Intelliflo partnership Partner IFA expertise
